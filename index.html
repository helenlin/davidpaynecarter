WE LOVE DAVID RIOS
ITP and IMA are fortunate to have an excellent faculty of professors who effortlessly inspire, challenge and educate students in the intersection of technology and the multidisciplinary arts. David carries the heart of our department—he is passionate about physical computing and his love for the field shows through his teaching. He recognizes the unique backgrounds and interests of each student and integrates them into his curriculum, making his approach to physical computing, experience design, and interface design feel both relevant and personal. He is our choice for the 2025 David Payne-Carter Award for Excellence in Teaching.
